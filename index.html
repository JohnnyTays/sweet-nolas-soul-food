About the Cook

"This Food Has a Story. And It Starts at the Table."

Every dish we make carries three generations of tradition — from the bayous of New Orleans, the kitchens of Arkansas, and the Sunday suppers of Alabama.

Sweet Nola's was built on a simple belief: real food takes real time, real ingredients, and real love. Nothing here comes from a box. Nothing here gets rushed. And nothing leaves our kitchen unless it's right.

Whether you're planning a family reunion, a birthday celebration, a repast, a corporate lunch, or any gathering where people matter — we bring the food that brings people together.

How It Works

Booking Sweet Nola's Is As Easy As 1-2-3

📋

1. Contact Us

Fill out our booking form below or call/text us directly. Tell us your event date, guest count, and what you're hungry for.

💳

2. Pay Your Deposit

A 50% deposit secures your date on the Sweet Nola's calendar. No deposit, no date — it's that simple. This protects your event and lets us go shopping for the freshest ingredients.

🍽️

3. We Show Up & Show Out

The day of your event, we arrive with everything ready. Hot food, real flavors, and the kind of spread your guests won't stop talking about. Balance is due on delivery.

All deposits are non-refundable. Full payment is due on the day of your event. Events booked within 7 days of the date require full payment upfront.
